Problems occurring during clean-up phases

  • Price gouging: Charging exorbitant fees for services that people have to pay, because they have no other option.
  • Fraud: In a chaotic environment, people make deals outside of normal legal protections.
    • For example, you get a contractor to do some work, he takes payment up front, says "I'll be back tomorrow" but never returns.
